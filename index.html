Guess the NHL Team Name

...a variation of the classic "Hangman" game!

Instructions:
In the game, you are going to try to guess the name of the NHL hockey team by guessing the different letters that spell that team's name.

  • Like Hangman, you have EIGHT guesses before the game ends.
  • Enter all letters as lowercase.
  • There are several team names with spaces in them. When guessing these, you may use the space bar.
  • You can continue play as long as you like - the game will keep a running tally of wins and losses during an uninterrupted session!
